About Me

My family immigrated to the the United States of America when I was nine months old with nothing but two suitcases. My father hoped to pursue what he saw as the American Dream. Thus, while growing up, my parents always stressed that I must follow my dreams and pursue whatever field I found myself interested in, which I have now found to be Computer Engineering. As long as I was driven and enjoyed the work I was doing, my parents would support me, and I could not have reached where I am now without their help and the values they have instilled in me.

I once saw what is now one of my favorite quotes on a coffee mug that I try to embody everyday:

"Do what you love. Love what you do."

It's that simple.
